The Formula for Adding VAT

To calculate the total price including tax, multiply the original net amount by 1 plus the tax rate percentage.

Reverse VAT: Calculating Backwards

Need to find the net price from a total? Use the Reverse VAT method. Divide the gross total by (1 + tax rate). For 20% VAT, divide by 1.20.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is a Reverse VAT Calculator?

It is a tool used to strip the tax portion away from a total price to see the original cost of a product or service.

Are the rates updated for 2025?

Yes, our calculator includes standard rates like 20% and 5%, but also allows for custom inputs to match any tax changes globally.

How do I calculate VAT in Excel?

Use =A1*1.2 to add 20% VAT, and =A1/1.2 to remove it.
